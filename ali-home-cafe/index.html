Recipes Passed Down For Generations

At Ali Home Cafe, we believe food has the power to bring comfort. Our kitchen in Porvorim prepares authentic Indian dishes modeled after the recipes prepared in our own home. We do not use chemical preservatives, coloring, or artificial flavor enhancers.

Every morning, our cooks hand-grind the spice blends, source the freshest cuts of premium meat, and start the slow dum cooking. That is why our Haleem is so rich, our Biryani rice is so fragrant, and our customers feel right at home.
