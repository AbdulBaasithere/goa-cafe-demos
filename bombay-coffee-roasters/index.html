OUR STORY

COFFEE WITH CHARACTER

Located in a beautifully restored building on Rua 31 de Janeiro, Bombay Coffee Roasters brings a unique industrial aesthetic to the historic heart of Panaji. We believe that coffee is more than just a drink; it's an experience.

Our space is designed to be a sanctuary for coffee lovers, where the aroma of freshly roasted beans meets the rugged charm of exposed brick and brass accents.
