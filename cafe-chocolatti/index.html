Cafe Chocolatti is a hidden gem tucked away in a beautiful garden on the main Fort Aguada Road. We specialize in the art of patisserie and chocolate making, bringing a touch of European elegance to the Goan coast.

Our cafe is the perfect spot for a long, lazy breakfast, a sophisticated lunch, or simply to indulge in a decadent piece of cake while enjoying the peace of our garden.
