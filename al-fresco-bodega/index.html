An Open-Air Sanctuary

Al Fresco by Bodega combines Goan sunshine with Portuguese architecture. Our café is designed as a peaceful, breezy courtyard retreat where you can slow down, read a book, or work under the shade of rustling palms.

Every plate we serve is crafted with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. From our home-baked pastries to our custom roasted estate coffee, we prioritize simple, honest, and delicious food that makes you feel good.
