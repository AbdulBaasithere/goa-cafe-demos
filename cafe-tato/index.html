For over a century, Cafe Tato has been the pulse of Panaji. Where generations of Goans meet for the city's most legendary breakfast and a timeless cup of tea.

Taste the Tradition

Panjim's Morning
Ritual

It started with a simple vision to serve honest, flavorful vegetarian food. Today, Cafe Tato is a cultural landmark, nestled in the shadow of the iconic Immaculate Conception Church.

Our walls have witnessed the evolution of Panaji, but our recipes remain untouched by time. We are the home of the authentic Goan morning experience.
