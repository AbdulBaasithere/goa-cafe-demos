The Spirit of the Shack

VJ's Shack was founded on a simple principle: good food shouldn't be complicated. We started as a small beach stall and have grown into a favorite spot for those who value authenticity over artifice.

Our kitchen is led by locals who know the secret to a perfect Goan masala. At VJ's, you're not just a customer; you're part of our beach family.
