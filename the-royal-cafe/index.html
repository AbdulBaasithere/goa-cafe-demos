A Feast For The Senses

Welcome to The Royal Cafe, a culinary haven where traditional Goan hospitality meets modern luxury. Situated on the vibrant Flea Market Road of Anjuna, our restaurant offers a relaxing retreat from the bustling beaches.

Our kitchen is dedicated to using the freshest catch of the day, locally-sourced organic vegetables, and time-honored family spice recipes to craft dishes that are both comforting and exciting.
