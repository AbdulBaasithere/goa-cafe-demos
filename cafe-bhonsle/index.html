Since 1920

Panaji's First Gathering Place

For more than ten decades, Cafe Bhonsle has stood as a symbol of Panaji's cultural history. Established in 1920, we started with a simple vision: to serve honest, flavorful, and vegetarian Goan snacks to the local community.

Over the years, the city has evolved, but our recipes have remained untouched. Our bustling kitchen still fills the air with the aroma of freshly fried samosas and simmering chickpeas, bringing families together over tables of warm memories.
