A Colorful Culinary Retreat

Orange Boom is more than just a cafe; it's a slice of Anjuna history. Tucked into the lush greenery on Flea Market Road, we've spent over a decade feeding the free spirits of Goa.

Our food is made with love, zero fuss, and heaps of flavor. Grab a sit-out under the shade of our mango trees, dive into our world-famous banana-caramel pancakes, and let the Goa slow-time take over.
