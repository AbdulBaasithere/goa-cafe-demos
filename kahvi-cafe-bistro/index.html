Fika & Hygge

The Art of the Coffee Break

In Sweden, 'Fika' is a daily ritual—a moment to slow down, catch up, and enjoy a warm drink with a sweet bake. KAHVI is designed to be your destination for Fika in Candolim. Our clean, wood-toned interiors create a calming mental break from the beach heat.

We source our coffee beans from clean single-origin estates, roasting them lightly in the Nordic style to preserve their unique terroir. Paired with our authentic open-face sandwiches and fresh cardamom rolls, it's a taste of slow northern living in tropical Goa.
