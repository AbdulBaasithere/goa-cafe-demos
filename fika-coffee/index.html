More Than a Coffee Break

The Art of the Fika

In Sweden, "Fika" isn't just a coffee break—it's a state of mind. It's about slowing down, savoring the moment, and connecting with those around you. We've brought this soulful concept to the vibrant streets of Panaji.

Located in the charming St. Thome area, Fika Coffee Co. is a tribute to craftsmanship and quality. From the precision of our pour-overs to the flaky layers of our croissants, everything we do is intentional.
