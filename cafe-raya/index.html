Our Story

A Neighborhood Sanctuary

Born from a passion for community and clean flavors, Café Raya has quickly become the heartbeat of Taleigao. We believe that a great cafe should be an extension of your living room—a place where conversation flows as easily as the coffee.

From our sourdough bakes to our signature pour-overs, every element of Raya is curated to help you slow down and savor the moment.
