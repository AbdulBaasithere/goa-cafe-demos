Unwind Under The Palms

Elephant Beach Cafe is Anjuna's premier spot for slow days and vibrant nights. Nestled along the famous Flea Market shoreline, we offer a dynamic blend of relaxed lounging and sunset partying.

Sink your toes in the soft sand, lounge on our custom wooden cabanas, and let our mixologists whip up your new favorite summer cocktail. As the golden hour approaches, witness the best sunset views in Goa.
