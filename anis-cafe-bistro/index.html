The Story of Ani's

Ani's Cafe & Bistro started with a simple idea: Panaji needed a place that combined great food with a truly relaxed, friendly atmosphere. We've poured our hearts into every corner of this bistro.

We're passionate about sourcing sustainable ingredients and creating a menu that has something for everyone—whether you're a coffee purist, a pizza lover, or just looking for a sweet treat.
