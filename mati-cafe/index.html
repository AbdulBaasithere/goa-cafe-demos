Rooted in Tradition

A Soulful Escape

Mati means "Soil" in Hindi, and our cafe is a tribute to the grounding, nurturing power of the earth. We believe that good food starts with honest ingredients and patient craftsmanship.

Our bakehouse bar concept brings together the comforting vibe of a bakery with the vibrant energy of a boutique bar. It's a space where you can start your day with a warm croissant and end it with a hand-crafted botanical cocktail.
