Nostalgia Baked Fresh Daily

For over two decades, Aunty Maria has served as Panaji's favorite neighborhood gathering spot. Located in the heart of the city at Hotel Fidalgo on 18th June Road, we blend cozy European bistro style with the warm, comforting nostalgia of a home kitchen.

Whether you're holding a morning business meeting over cappuccinos, enjoying a lazy Sunday brunch with flaky pastries, or stopping by for a midnight slice of cheesecake, Aunty Maria is always ready to welcome you with delicious eats and timeless charm.
