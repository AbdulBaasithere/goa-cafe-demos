The Craft Behind
Every Bite

Larder & Folk is our tribute to the art of the deli. In a world of fast food, we choose the long road. Our sourdough takes 30 hours. Our pickles are fermented for weeks. Our sauces are made from scratch daily.

We are a neighborhood space where the ingredients tell the story and every guest is treated like family. Welcome to the folk.
