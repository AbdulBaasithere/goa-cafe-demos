Elegance in Every Detail

Located near the serene Colva Church, Mon Petit Frère is more than just a cafe. It's a tribute to the timeless charm of French hospitality. Our bistro combines an elegant atmosphere with a relaxed Goan vibe, creating the perfect setting for any occasion.

Whether you're looking for a quiet morning coffee, a sophisticated lunch, or a romantic evening dinner, our team is dedicated to providing an unforgettable experience of taste and style.
