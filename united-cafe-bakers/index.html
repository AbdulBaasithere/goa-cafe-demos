Our Story

Why Guests Love United Cafe & Bakers

At United Cafe & Bakers in Arpora, every morning begins with the warm scent of fresh-baked pastries and breads. Rated 4.8⭐ by 51+ loyal guests, our bakehouse draws from Goa's rich Portuguese baking tradition — from flaky pao and custard pastel de nata to celebration cakes and artisan loaves. Everything is baked from scratch, with love, every single day.
